my story

I'm originally from the U.S. but I've been living and working in Spain for over ten years. In that time I've mostly been an 👩‍🏫 English teacher or teaching assistant. (I know, what a cliché.) This year I decided to finally make a change in my professional life.

In May I enrolled in a three month intensive web development course. It was supposed to be in person but with current world affairs, I ended up getting a fully remote experience. The course was most definitely intense 😅 but I enjoyed the fast pace and the time went by quickly. In just three months I learned so much and had a ton of fun doing it, even if I slept less than ever before in my life. 💤

Now that I've finished training, I'm looking for my first developer job. It's not easy, let me tell ya. Most companies want employees with years of experience, even if the offer says it's for a junior developer. So to continue expanding my knowledge, as well as to keep myself sane, I study 📚 and create 💥 every day. You can see what I've been up to by visiting my GitHub, CodePen, or Twitter page.
